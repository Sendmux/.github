# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Sendmux spaces should be practical, respectful, and focused on improving the software and documentation.

Expected behaviour:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good intent, then ask for clarification when something is unclear.
- Keep issues and pull requests on topic.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Avoid sharing private information without consent.

Unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or customer data.
- Spam, promotional noise, or repeated off-topic comments.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to [contact@sendmux.ai](mailto:contact@sendmux.ai). Include links, screenshots, and context where useful, but do not include secrets or private customer data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, restrict participation, or block accounts when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
